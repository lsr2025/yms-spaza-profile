The Challenge

A Sector in Dual Crisis

⚠️

Public Health Crisis

Food-borne illness outbreaks are escalating in township retail. Spaza shops often lack Certificate of Acceptability, safe food temperatures, and basic hygiene infrastructure — threatening community health.

🏦

Financial Exclusion

Thousands of viable spaza businesses are locked out of the R500M Spaza Shop Support Fund — not due to ineligibility, but because they can't navigate the compliance and documentation requirements.

📋

EHP Capacity Gap

Municipal Environmental Health Practitioners face a massive inspection backlog. Without triage tools, critical violators remain undetected while EHPs waste capacity on low-risk premises.

🗺️

Data Vacuum

Enterprise iLembe has no verified spatial or economic data on informal traders. Without this foundation, targeted interventions and evidence-based policy are impossible.

02 / 12
The Solution

One Platform.
Four Strategic Outcomes.

01

Operationalize Regulation R638 Compliance

Translate complex food safety law into binary checklists for 500 non-expert field agents — flagging critical risks before they become outbreaks.

02

Enable NEF Funding Readiness

Pre-screen shops against SSSF criteria — citizenship, licensing, banking, SARS — creating a verified pipeline of funding-ready enterprises.

03

Augment EHP Capacity via Triage

SEF agents as "compliance scouts" surface high-risk shops, allowing scarce EHPs to direct their statutory powers where they matter most.

04

Build Sovereign District-Level Data

Capture verified GPS, business, and health records — giving iLembe a proprietary dataset to drive economic formalization across South Africa.

Technical Architecture

Secure by Design. Offline by Default.

Android · WatermelonDB / SQLite · AES-256 local encryption · Delta-sync engine · IMEI device binding

AWS / Azure · S3/Blob photo storage · TLS 1.3 in transit · REST APIs · GeoJSON spatial layers
