About the Organisation

Meaningful Empowerment.
Measurable Impact.

Yami Mine Solutions (YMS) was founded with intention. "Yami" means Mine — a name that reflects ownership, accountability, and craftsmanship. Our solutions are not generic interventions; they are carefully structured, custom-made solutions designed with passion, skill, and a clear end goal in mind.

While many associate our name with the mining sector, our roots are firmly grounded in socio-economic development, enterprise growth, and empowerment-driven implementation. We operate at the intersection of development finance, enterprise implementation, and sector-specific impact delivery.

We bring structured governance, compliance discipline, and measurable outcomes to every programme we manage. Empowerment is not a slogan — it is about building sustainable enterprises, enabling participation in the economy, and ensuring communities are stronger because we were there.

"Yami Mine Solutions is more than a name. It is a commitment to ownership, to excellence, and to meaningful impact."
Programme Objectives

What We Set Out to Achieve

The programme aims to strengthen the sustainability and compliance of local spaza shop enterprises through targeted enterprise development support.

01
Regulatory Compliance
Support regulatory compliance including municipal trading permits and environmental health requirements, ensuring businesses operate within the law.
02
Food Safety & Hygiene
Improve food safety and hygiene practices within participating businesses, raising standards and building consumer trust across township retail.
03
Business Development
Provide practical business support — stock management, store organisation, and operational efficiency for sustainable long-term growth.
04
Funding Access
Assist entrepreneurs with NEF Spaza Shop Support Programme applications, connecting businesses to development capital and funding pipelines.
Strategic Pillars

Four Pillars of Structured Impact

YMS operates across four interconnected service pillars, each designed to create sustainable, measurable development outcomes.

1
Enterprise & Supplier Development
We build resilient enterprises through structured ESD frameworks, small business incubation, capacity development, and funding readiness. Our model strengthens supplier pipelines and enhances inclusive procurement outcomes.
2
Social Employment & Public Stimulus
We design and implement large-scale public employment and stimulus programmes with structured governance and measurable impact, ensuring public funding translates into sustainable economic participation.
3
Intermediary Fund & Grant Management
We administer enterprise development funds with robust financial controls and DFI-aligned governance standards, ensuring transparent disbursement, monitoring, and impact reporting.
4
LED & Transformation Advisory
YMS partners with municipalities and corporates to design LED strategies, integrate Mining Social and Labour Plans, and build SMME development frameworks aligned to national priorities.
